## BANGLADESH LEGAL TOOL: MLPA 2012 — WIDE REACH

Bangladesh does NOT have a standalone Benami Act (unlike India).
However, **Money Laundering Prevention Act 2012, Section 14** is broader:

> The court may freeze **"any property connected to"** a money laundering charge.
> This is NOT limited to property in the accused's own name.

**Key point for family assets:**
- Any asset in Sadia Chowdhury's name: No protection — she is a co-accused.
- Any asset in a child's/sibling's name: Subject to attachment if CID can show
  the beneficial owner is Sikder (nominee arrangement). The nominee must prove
  in court within 30 days that they are a bona fide owner and Sikder has no interest.
- **The burden is on the nominee to prove innocence, not on the state to prove guilt.**
